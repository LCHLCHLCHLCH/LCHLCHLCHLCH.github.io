For the past three years,it's—

 Unbelievable that time has flown by.

 Could I forget the memory?No,I will--

 Keep them in memory.

 

 Youth marks the years of struggling,

 Opening my eyes to the world.

 Under the sun of youth,I will keep going.

 

 Never will I look behind,nor--

 Elasping time would come back.

 With a buring heart,I--

 Hope I could go on with courage.

 Oh,my dearest new hope school!

 Please remember my preasence,

 Enabing me to chase a brighter future.
